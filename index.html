Welcome

Welcome to my blog! It is a labour of love that I want to share with YOU!

Please enjoy reading about all things related to family, online tree building, ancestral research, and genetic genealogy. My desire is to simplify the process for you as well as share tips and tricks that I have learned. By sharing my journey to find my biological family, I hope I can inspire you to be brave enough to do the same.

In my narcissism related blog posts, my goal is to make you aware of red flags and the dangers of narcissists. They are out there and they are harmful! If you are reading and can relate, please reach out and I will share some helpful resources with you.

In my family blog posts, I intend to share some light-hearted musings about my life and my tribe, who I ADORE.


All my love,MamaJo
